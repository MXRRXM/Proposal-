The Immersive Lab: Collaborative Intelligence Hub

360° Learning environment for K-12 and group activities

Overview

The Immersive Lab is a high-utility, shared digital environment designed to be the heartbeat of the school’s innovation strategy. Rather than a single-purpose room, it is a multi-activity space that adapts to every subject, age group, and learning style. By projecting a seamless 360° digital skin across the walls, we turn the physical classroom into a giant, interactive tablet.

Interactive & Tactile Engagement

  • 360° Touch-Screen Integration: The space is not just for viewing; it is for doing. The entire perimeter can be enabled with touchscreen, allowing students to manipulate data, draw on 360° canvases, and interact with digital objects directly on the walls.
  • Across Versatility: One hour the space is a deep-sea research station; the next, it is a collaborative "War Room" for student council debates or a zen-space for mindfulness and well-being.

Institutional Impact: Safety, Inclusion & Branding

"Ensuring a Secure and Marketable Innovation Strategy"

A Walled Garden: Safeguarding & Moderation

In a K-12 environment, student safety is our primary objective. Unlike open-platform technologies, the Immersive Lab is a "Walled Garden" ecosystem.

  • Curated Content: All 360° environments and AI-generated assets are filtered through school-safe protocols.
  • Teacher-Controlled: The educator remains the "Orchestrator," with full control over the visual and auditory environment at all times via a centralized tablet.
  • Data Privacy: All student engagement metrics are handled locally and securely, adhering to global institutional data protection standards.

Inclusive Innovation: Supporting SEND & Universal Design

The Immersive Lab is an "Accessible Innovation" space, designed to be inclusive of students with diverse learning needs:

  • Sensory-Friendly Learning: The open-air nature of the lab is ideal for students who may experience sensory overload or physical discomfort with traditional headsets.
  • Physical Accessibility: The lab is 100% wheelchair accessible, ensuring that students with physical disabilities have the same "front-row seat" to global exploration as their peers.
  • Neurodiversity: The high-visual, kinetic nature provides a powerful alternative for auditory and visual learners who struggle with traditional text-heavy pedagogy.

Technical Infrastructure

"A Multi-Activity, Touch-Enabled Environment for Active Learning"

The Immersive Lab is a high-performance, free-standing 4m x 4m structure designed for deep institutional impact. It is powered by the industry-leading Igloo Core Engine and specialized Immersive Media Players (IMP), ensuring a seamless, high-fidelity experience.

  • Projection System: A fully-immersive system featuring six high-lumen projectors. Options include 4K resolution for the highest quality projected image achievable in the industry, delivering unmatched clarity for simulation and VR.
  • 360° Sound: Integrated 4.0 surround sound (wired or networked) provides a multi-channel auditory experience that moves with the content.
  • Tactile Interaction: Controlled via a Windows-based touch control panel, allowing teachers and students to navigate environments with the same ease as a tablet.
  • Customizable Design: The structure features fully-branded fabric outer skins and an aluminium frame, allowing the lab to be physically integrated into the school’s aesthetic.
